Privacy Policy for THE FIT 24
At THE FIT 24, we understand that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or use our fitness center or our website (www.thefit24.in).
Compliance

This Privacy Policy is published in compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including but not limited to:
Section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000
Regulation 4 of the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules")
Regulation 3 of the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021

What Does This Privacy Policy Cover?
This Privacy Policy covers our treatment of personally identifiable information ("personal information") that we gather when you are availing, accessing, or using our services, including visiting our fitness center, using our website, or availing third-party products and services through our platform.
This policy does not cover the practices of companies that we do not own or control, or individuals that we do not employ.

Information We Collect
Information You Provide to Us:

We receive and store any information you knowingly provide to us, including:
Personal information collected at the time of your registration on our website or at the fitness center, including your name, email address, gender, birth date, height, and location.
Information related to your fitness goals, workout preferences, and health-related data.
Bank details for payment processing.
Any other information you provide to us through forms, surveys, or interactions with our staff.
Information Collected Automatically:
When you interact with our website or use our services, we automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information, and the pages you request. Cookies are identifiers we transfer to your computer or mobile device that allow us to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our website are visited and by how many people.
Purpose and Use of Information
We may use your personal information for the following purposes:
To provide and improve our services, including optimizing the operation of our fitness center and website.
To contact you regarding your membership, services, or promotions.
For general business and operating purposes, such as administration, management, and responding to requests.
For payment, billing, and invoicing purposes.
To generate aggregated data to understand user preferences and trends.
To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or security risks.

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not rent or sell your personal information in personally identifiable form to anyone. We share your personal information in personally identifiable form with third parties only as described below:
Affiliated Businesses: In certain situations, businesses or third-party websites we're affiliated with may provide services to you through our platform. We will share your personal information with these affiliated businesses only to the extent that it is related to such transactions or services.
Agents: We employ other companies and people to perform tasks on our behalf and need to share your information with them to provide services to you.
User Submissions: Any content or personal information that you voluntarily disclose online in a manner other users can view (on discussion boards, in messages, and chat areas, etc.) becomes publicly available and can be collected and used by others.
Business Transfers: If we buy or sell assets, customer information may be one of the business assets transferred.
Protection of THE FIT 24 and Others: We may release personal information when we believe in good faith that release is necessary to comply with laws, enforce our conditions of use, or protect the rights, property, or safety of THE FIT 24, our employees, our users, or others.
Security of Your Personal Information
Your account is protected by a password for your privacy and security. You need to prevent unauthorized access to your account and personal information by selecting and protecting your password appropriately and limiting access to your computer or device.
We endeavor to protect the privacy of your account and other personal information we hold in our records, but we cannot guarantee complete security. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time.
